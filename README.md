# ChefAI

> **Your personal AI-powered kitchen companion** — manage your ingredients, set your preferences, and get custom recipes generated just for you.

---

## Overview

ChefAI bridges the gap between what's in your fridge and what ends up on your plate. Instead of searching endless recipe sites and settling for something that almost matches what you have, ChefAI looks at your actual ingredients, respects your dietary needs, and generates tailored recipes on demand — complete with step-by-step cooking instructions and nutritional breakdowns.

---

## Features

### Ingredient Management
Keep a live, organized inventory of everything in your kitchen.

- **Add** new ingredients 
- **Edit** existing entries 
- **Remove** ingredients you've used up or discarded
- **Organize** ingredients into custom lists (e.g. *Carbs*, *Protein*)

### Custom Ingredient Lists
Group your ingredients any way that makes sense for your kitchen.

- **Create** named lists to categorize your stock
- **Edit** list names and rearrange contents
- **Delete** lists you no longer need
- Move ingredients between lists freely

### Recipe Parameters
Fine-tune what kind of recipe you're looking for before generating.

| Parameter | Options                                             |
|---|-----------------------------------------------------|
| **Dietary Preference** | Kosher · Halal · Vegan · Vegetarian · None          |
| **Difficulty** | Easy · Medium · Hard · Very Hard                    |
| **Food Type** | Salad · Dessert · Soup · Grilled · Baked · and more |

### AI Recipe Generation
When you're ready, hit **Make** and let ChefAI do the work.

- Generate **up to 5 recipes per day**
- Recipes are matched to your current ingredient inventory and selected parameters
- Each generated recipe includes:
  - **Full cooking instructions** — clear, step-by-step guidance
  - **Nutritional values** — calories, allergies, and key nutrients
  - **Download option** — save the recipe to your device as pdf for offline use

---

## Getting Started

1. **Add your ingredients** — start building your kitchen inventory
2. **Organize into lists** — group items however works for you
3. **Set your preferences** — pick your dietary style, difficulty, and food type
4. **Hit Make** — receive personalized recipes based on what you actually have
5. **Cook & enjoy** — follow the instructions, check the nutrition info, or download to keep
